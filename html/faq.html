Health FAQ

  • Are there any teas I should avoid while pregnant or breastfeeding?

    For personal health questions, it is always best to speak to your healthcare provider. All of our teas include a detailed ingredients list written on the side of each box, and on each tea’s individual online product’s page. This will provide you with the necessary information needed to ask your doctor on whether specific blends or ingredients are right for you.

  • Where can I find a complete list of ingredients for each tea?

    All of our teas include a detailed ingredients list written on the side of each box, and on each tea’s individual online product’s page.

  • Where can I find more info about allergies?

    Any queries with regards to a severe allergy, we recommend that you talk to your doctor before drinking our teas.

  • Do your teas contain sugar or calories?

    Most of our teas contain virtually no sugar, carbohydrates or calories. However, do understand that it is extremely difficult to pinpoint the nutritional facts on tea since it is a natural product, like a fruit or vegetable. Calories and carbohydrates cannot be calculated due to the minuscule amount found in tea leaves. Without milk or sugar added to tea, tea contains very little calories.

  • How much caffeine is there in a cup of tea?

    It’s hard to say for sure due to a ton of factors, such as water temperature and steeping time, that can change the amount of caffeine in a cup. Although it’s virtually impossible to measure the exact amount of caffeine in every cup, all our teas have been tested and rated with a general rating system as below:

    ♣ Medium caffeine
    ♣ High caffeine

TEA FAQ

  • Where are your teas blended?

    Our team blends all of our teas by hand in small batch quantities at our Singapore atelier

  • What makes Gryphon Tea different from other tea companies?

    All our teas are inspired by various Asian heritages and cultures

  • Can I steep my tea more than once?

    In many cases, the flavour profile will be unique with each steeping. Re-steeping is not necessarily recommended for tisanes. For best results, you should also only re-steep within the first couple of hours of the first infusion.

  • Why do your teas contain flavouring?

    Rest assured that only natural flavourings are used in our blends. These flavourings are added to give each of our teas its unique taste profile. However, if you’re not keen on flavourings, we have plenty of other unflavoured blends available.

  • How long does your tea stay fresh?

    Although tea does not go bad, it does become stale over time. We generally recommend drinking any of our teas within 2 years of purchase or before best date indicated.

  • What is the best way for me store my loose tea?

    We recommend that you store your loose tea in an air-tight container, kept away from direct sunlight and in a cool and dry environment.
