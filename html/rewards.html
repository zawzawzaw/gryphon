FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

How much is each point worth?
Each point is worth 10 cents, therefore 10 points will be worth $1. It’s like a 10% cashback.
What payment types do you accept?
We’d love to hear your feedback about how our products worked for you. Simply click on “Add Review” located underneath or to the right of the product image and share your thoughts. Once the review has been approved by the team, customers will be able to see the rewards on their account.
Note: We reserve the right to delete reviews that are derogatory, offensive or includes the usage of foul language, at our own discretion. Under such circumstances, points will not be dispersed. Total maximum number of points given will be based on total number of items purchased by customer.
Can I use the accumulated points to offset purchases made during promotional periods?
Though customers cannot accumulate points during promotions, customers will be able to offset and spend their points when purchasing discounted items.click on “Add Review” located underneath or to the right of the product image and share your thoughts. Once the review has been approved by the team.
How do I refer a friend?
Simply invite the friend you wish to invite by adding their email into the “to” field, combined with a simple message in the box below. Once done, press “invite”. When your referred friend creates an account with us, you will be notified by email and also, receive the points.
How do I track my points?
To stay updated on your available points, simply login to your Gryphon Tea account.
Do the points have an expiry date?
All points accumulated would expire 1 year from the date of registration. After a year, all unused points would be void.
