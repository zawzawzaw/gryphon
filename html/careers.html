Join Us

As a leader in the tea industry, we offer an exciting and challenging working environment with plenty of opportunities.

More than just tea

We do more than serve tea. We serve our customers, and that means we are incredibly dedicated to providing the best possible service and experience for our loyal patrons. To understand and satisfy their diverse needs, we work together like a family. And as a member of our family you will have all the tools and support you need to realise your goals. Then we want you to create new goals, and we will help you achieve them too.

why join us

ROOM TO GROW

We’re always looking to grow and that means lots of opportunities to advance and stake out new ground. If you’re independent, self-starting and motivated, we want you. Bring your ideas and energy, and you will love being part of our team.

A BALANCED LIFE

All work and no play makes for a very boring day. We love to have fun and build relationships during meals, outings and trips. And that also means you go home when you’re supposed to, because we get things done on time. Over-time is for the other guys.

TAKE RISKS

Failure is an ingredient for eventual success. Take risks, learn from mistakes, and push on. We want you to experiment. Mistakes don’t scare us – they tell us you’re going to eventually make something happen!

TAKE CHARGE

Think like a leader. Take ownership. During meetings, don’t sit in – we want you to lean in. Whether it’s a project or you’re just walking by, stop and let us know what you’re thinking. With us, you’re more than just an employee. You have something important to contribute.
